Undergraduate Courses offered in VESIT
VISION
To produce globally competent and socially responsible engineers capable of independently solving problems with a sound knowledge of engineering in Electronics and Computer Science.

MISSION
To prepare students in the field of engineering in Electronics and Computer Science for lifelong learning. To develop professionals who can think critically and provide cost-effective solutions to solve local and global issues using knowledge of Electronics and Computer Science. To inculcate a sense of being job providers than job seekers with due consideration to ethical and social values.
